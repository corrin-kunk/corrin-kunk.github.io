Weekly Meal Ideas

Simple meal plans for busy families with picky eaters.

Sample Weekly Plan

Use this as a starting point and swap meals based on what your child already likes.

Monday

  • Breakfast: Greek Yogurt Pancakes
  • Lunch: Turkey Roll-Ups
  • Dinner: One-Pan Cheesy Chicken and Rice
  • Snack: Fruit and Yogurt

Tuesday

  • Breakfast: Egg Muffin Bites
  • Lunch: Cheese Quesadilla with fruit
  • Dinner: 15-Minute Creamy Mac & Cheese
  • Snack: Apple Slices and Peanut Butter

Wednesday

  • Breakfast: Toast with eggs or yogurt
  • Lunch: Turkey Roll-Ups
  • Dinner: Homemade pizza night
  • Snack: Veggies with dip

Thursday

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with banana
  • Lunch: Crackers, cheese, turkey, and fruit lunch box
  • Dinner: Crockpot Chicken Nachos
  • Snack: Smoothie

Friday

  • Breakfast: Banana Oatmeal Muffins
  • Lunch: Peanut butter sandwich with fruit
  • Dinner: 15-Minute Creamy Mac & Cheese
  • Snack: Popcorn or trail mix

Weekend Ideas

  • Breakfast: Fluffy Greek Yogurt Pancakes
  • Lunch: Build-your-own snack plates
  • Dinner: Crockpot Chicken Nachos
  • Snack: Banana Oatmeal Muffins
