Pacific Rhododendron

Scientific Name: Rhododendron macrophyllum D. Don ex G. Don

Common Alternate Names of the Plant:

  • California Rosebay
  • California Rhododendron
  • Coast Rhododendron
  • Bigleaf Rhododendron

Size

Pacific Rhododendron is a large shrub that can grow up to 25 feet tall.

It has large, leathery leaves that can be up to 3-9 inches long and 1-3 inches wide.

Each flower measures about 1-1.5 inches long and features a narrow, bell-shaped corolla that is deeply devided into five wavy lobes.
