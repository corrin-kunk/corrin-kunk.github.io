Chocolate Chip Cookies

Prep Time: 10 min

Cook Time: 8 min

Total Time: 30 min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup salted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Cream butter and sugars together until combined.
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla until smooth.
  5. Mix in dry ingredients until combined.
  6. Stir in chocolate chips.
  7. Roll dough into balls and place on baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 8–10 minutes until lightly golden.
  9. Cool for a few minutes before transferring to a rack.
