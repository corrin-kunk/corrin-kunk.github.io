Banana Bread

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 1 hr

Total Time: 1 hr 10 mins

Ingredients

  • 3 very ripe bananas
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup walnuts
  • 1/2 cup raisins

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease and flour a loaf pan.
  2. Lightly toast walnuts in a skillet, then chop and let cool.
  3. Cream butter and sugar together in a mixing bowl.
  4. Mash bananas and add to mixture with eggs, stirring until combined.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk flour, baking soda, and salt, then add to batter.
  6. Add vanilla, walnuts, and raisins. Mix well and pour into pan.
  7. Bake for 55–60 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool before serving.
